Glosso Studio is a professional, offline-first pronunciation training application. It leverages advanced phonetic recognition models to provide real-time feedback on your spoken English.

Key Features:
- Real-time Phonetic Assessment: Uses the Allosaurus eng2102 model to analyze your speech with high precision.
- Offline-First: All recognition and curriculum data are stored locally for privacy and performance.
- Structured Curriculum: Six difficulty tiers ranging from Beginner to Mastery.
- Dynamic Content: On-demand download of curriculum databases.
- Progress Tracking: Track your mastery score and maintain daily practice streaks.
